About Ekoy’s Homemade Desserts

Welcome to Ekoy’s Homemade Desserts, where we bring you the sweetest treats made with love and passion! We specialize in Filipino favorites like Buko Pandan, Mango Graham, and more.

Each dessert is crafted using fresh, high-quality ingredients, ensuring a delightful and satisfying experience in every bite.

Perfect for celebrations, gatherings, or simply treating yourself to something special!

We started this business on 2020, online businesses was trending because of pandemic. Most people cannot go out because of the virus, people only go out for food or to do something else, but its hard to go outside and most people just order food online. so we decided to start our own online business, Ekoys desserts. Anyone can order and we would deliver the food immidiately.
